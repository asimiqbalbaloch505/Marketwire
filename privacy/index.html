Privacy Policy & Data Sovereignty

Last Substantial Revision: February 18, 2026

At MarketWire24, we believe financial intelligence should not come at the cost of personal liberty. This policy outlines how we handle your data in accordance with GDPR (2026 Edition), CCPA/CPRA, and the Digital Asset CLARITY Act.

1. Data We Collect

We minimize data collection to the strictly necessary "Protocol Essentials":

  • Technical Identifiers: IP addresses and browser fingerprints used solely to prevent DDoS attacks on our high-speed news feed.
  • Usage Metadata: Tracking which asset classes (e.g., BTC, NASDAQ) you view to prioritize your local terminal cache.
  • Secure Form Data: Any information provided via our Contact Form or Whistleblower Tip-line is encrypted via AES-256 at rest.

2. Cookies & Advertising (RevBid)

MarketWire24 is supported by the RevBid Advertising Network. Our ad partners use cookies to ensure that you see institutional-grade financial offers rather than generic consumer goods.

3. AI & Automated Decision-Making (ADMT)

In 2026, we utilize Agentic AI to categorize news. We do NOT use AI to profile individual users for credit-worthiness or insurance eligibility. Our AI processing is limited to:

  • Content recommendation based on ticker-interest.
  • Automated translation of global market alerts.

4. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA 2026)

You have the legal right to:

  • Right to Know: Request a copy of all data MarketWire24 holds on you.
  • Right to Delete: Request immediate erasure of your data ("The Right to be Forgotten").
  • GPC Signal Support: Our servers automatically honor Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals to opt you out of data sales.

To exercise these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@marketwire24.com.

Trust & Safety

We are audited annually for compliance with the 2026 Cyber-Audit Standards. We do not store credit card or sensitive financial passwords on our servers.
